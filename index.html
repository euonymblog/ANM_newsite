Aide Népal Magnoac

"Better to light a candle than complain about the dark"

After the devastating earthquakes in 2015, a group of friends got together to raise funds to rebuild the school in Taruka, which had been destroyed. Since then, strong bonds of friendship have been forged between the Magnoac area of the French Pyrenees and communities in the Himalayas.

Quiz Night 6 April

Our next event will be a Quiz Night on 6 April in the Salle de Fete in Monleon-Magnoac. Entry is 5 euro per table, and a table can be up to 4 people.
